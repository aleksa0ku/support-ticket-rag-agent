# Refund Policy

## Monthly subscriptions

Monthly subscription charges are non-refundable, but you can cancel at any
time to prevent future charges. Cancelling stops renewal; you keep paid
features until the end of the current billing period.

## Annual subscriptions

If you're on an annual plan and cancel within 14 days of the charge, you're
eligible for a full refund. Requests after the 14-day window are evaluated
case-by-case and are not guaranteed — a support agent will review the account
history before deciding.

## How to request a refund

Go to Settings > Plan > Billing History, find the charge, and click "Request
refund." This creates a ticket that a billing specialist reviews within 2
business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and can
take 5–10 business days to appear depending on your bank.

## Accidental duplicate charges

If you believe you were charged twice for the same billing period, this is
usually a payment processor retry rather than an actual double charge —
check Settings > Plan > Billing History first to confirm whether one or two
line items actually settled. If two charges did settle, this requires a
human billing specialist to investigate and reverse, since it may involve
your specific payment processor and account history.

# Invoices and Payment Methods

## Viewing and downloading invoices

All invoices are available under Settings > Plan > Billing History as PDF
downloads. Invoices are generated automatically within a few minutes of each
successful charge.

## Updating your payment method

Go to Settings > Plan > Payment Method > Edit. Cloudbox accepts major credit
cards and, on annual Business plans, ACH/wire transfer. Updating your card
does not trigger an immediate charge; it takes effect on the next billing
date.

## Failed payments

If a renewal charge fails, we retry automatically over 3 attempts across 7
days. You'll get an email each time. If all retries fail, the account is
downgraded to Free at the end of the retry window, but files are not
deleted — see [storage limits](storage-limits) for what happens to files
above the Free tier's 5 GB cap.

## Adding a VAT/Tax ID

Business plan customers in the EU/UK can add a VAT number under Settings >
Plan > Billing Details so it appears on invoices. This does not change your
price automatically; tax handling depends on your local jurisdiction rules
and may require a human review for edge cases (e.g. reverse-charge
eligibility).

# Cloudbox Plans and Pricing

Cloudbox offers four plans:

| Plan       | Storage | Price (monthly) | Price (annual) |
|------------|---------|------------------|-----------------|
| Free       | 5 GB    | $0               | $0              |
| Plus       | 200 GB  | $9.99            | $99/yr          |
| Family     | 1 TB (shared, up to 6 members) | $16.99 | $169/yr |
| Business   | 2 TB per user, unlimited if 3+ users | $20/user | $204/user/yr |

## Switching plans

Go to Settings > Plan > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and you
are charged a prorated amount for the rest of the current billing cycle.
Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle — you keep
your current storage limit until then.

## Annual vs. monthly billing

Switching from monthly to annual billing can be done at any time from
Settings > Plan and takes effect immediately, with a prorated credit applied
for unused monthly time. Switching from annual to monthly billing takes
effect at the next renewal date.

## What happens if I go over my storage limit?

You can't upload new files once you're over your limit, but existing files
remain safe and accessible. You'll see a banner prompting you to upgrade or
free up space. See [Managing storage limits](storage-limits) for details.

## Family and Business plan seats

On Family and Business plans, the plan owner can add or remove members from
Settings > Members. Removing a member frees up their allotted storage but
does not delete their personal files — those become inaccessible to the
group but are retained per our [account deletion and data retention
policy](account-deletion).

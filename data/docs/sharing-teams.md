# Shared Folders and Team Collaboration

## Creating a shared folder

Right-click a folder and choose "Share folder," then enter the email
addresses of people to invite. Invitees get an email with a link to accept;
once accepted, the folder appears in their Cloudbox and syncs like any
other.

## Permission levels

- **Editor** — can add, edit, and delete files.
- **Viewer** — can view and download but not modify.
- **Co-owner** (Business plan) — can also manage other members' permissions.

Change a member's permission level from the folder's "Manage access" panel.

## Removing someone from a shared folder

Open "Manage access," find the person, and click "Remove." Their copy of the
folder is removed from their account, but they keep any files they'd already
downloaded separately outside the sync folder.

## Ownership transfer

If the folder owner leaves the organization or deletes their account,
ownership can be transferred to another editor from "Manage access >
Transfer ownership" (Business plan) before the account is closed. Without
this step, an orphaned shared folder becomes read-only for remaining members
until a workspace admin reassigns it — this specific reassignment must be
done by support since it touches account-level permissions.

## Activity log

Business plan shared folders keep a 90-day activity log (who added, edited,
or deleted which files) under "Manage access > Activity."

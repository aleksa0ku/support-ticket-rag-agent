# Closing Your Account and Data Retention

## Exporting your data first

Before closing your account, download everything via Settings > Account >
Export Data, which packages your files into a downloadable zip. This can
take a while to prepare for large accounts — you'll get an email when it's
ready.

## Closing your account

Settings > Account > Close Account requires confirming your password and
typing "DELETE" to confirm. This immediately cancels any active
subscription (no further charges) and schedules your files for deletion.

## Data retention after closing

Files are permanently deleted from our servers 30 days after account
closure, matching the trash retention window. Within those 30 days, contact
support to reverse the closure and restore full access; after 30 days,
deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed by anyone, including support.

## Closing an account that owns shared folders

If you own folders shared with others, closing your account converts those
folders to read-only for other members unless ownership is transferred
first (see [Shared folders](sharing-teams)). We recommend transferring
ownership before closing the account to avoid disrupting collaborators.

## Legal holds and compliance requests

Requests related to legal holds, GDPR/CCPA data requests, or law enforcement
requests are handled outside the normal account-closure flow and must go to
our privacy/legal team rather than general support.

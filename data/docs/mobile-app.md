# Using the Cloudbox Mobile App

## Installing

The Cloudbox app is available on the App Store (iOS 15+) and Google Play
(Android 9+). Sign in with your existing Cloudbox account — no separate
mobile account needed.

## Camera upload

Enable Settings > Camera Upload in the app to automatically back up new
photos and videos to a `Camera Uploads` folder in your Cloudbox. You can
restrict this to Wi-Fi only under the same settings to avoid mobile data
charges.

## Offline access

Tap the three-dot menu on any file and choose "Make available offline" to
download it for offline viewing. Offline files sync updates automatically
the next time you're online.

## Mobile app isn't syncing new photos

Check that: Camera Upload is enabled, the app has photo library permission
(Settings > Privacy on iOS, App Permissions on Android), and background app
refresh isn't disabled for Cloudbox. On Android, some manufacturers'
battery-optimization features aggressively kill background apps — add
Cloudbox to the battery optimization exclusion list.

## Widescreen/tablet support

The mobile app supports split-screen and drag-and-drop on iPadOS and
Android tablets for moving files between Cloudbox and other apps.

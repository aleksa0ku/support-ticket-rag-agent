# Getting Started with Cloudbox

Cloudbox is a cloud file storage and sync service. This guide covers creating
your account and getting your first files synced.

## Creating an account

1. Go to cloudbox.example.com/signup and enter your email and password.
2. Verify your email by clicking the link we send you (check spam if it
   doesn't arrive within a few minutes).
3. Choose a plan. You can start on the Free plan (5 GB) and upgrade later
   from Settings > Plan.

## Installing the desktop app

Download the installer for Windows or macOS from the Downloads page. After
installing, sign in with your Cloudbox credentials. A `Cloudbox` folder is
created in your home directory — anything you drag into it syncs to the
cloud automatically.

## Your first sync

Files placed in the Cloudbox folder upload automatically. Sync status is
shown as an icon overlay on the file: a green check means fully synced, a
blue spinning arrow means upload/download in progress. Initial sync of a
large existing folder can take a while depending on your connection —
progress is shown in the menu-bar/tray app.
